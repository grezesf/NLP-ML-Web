I suggest you read Letters to a Young Mathematician by Ian Stwart. It describes informally what mathematics is and what mathematicians do. When you have done some college level mathematics already and wish to survey the branches of mathematics, you may take a look at The Princeton Companion to Mathematics edited by Fields medallist Timothy Gowers.
