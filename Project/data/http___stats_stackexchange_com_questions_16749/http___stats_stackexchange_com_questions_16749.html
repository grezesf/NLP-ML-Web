How to measure the effect of users' participation in forums?

up vote 0 down vote favorite

My problem is how to measure the effect of user's participation in forums. I calculate the user's participation as the number of discussions posted. My hypothesis is that a user's participation is not independent of each other and that the user's participation depends on the group ie that user's behaviour change based on the group.

For example my data will be like this: 

| users | Forum 1 | Forum 2 | Forum 3 |
| u1    | 10      | 5       | NA      |
| u2    | 6       | NA      | 4       |
| u3    | 5       | 2       | 3       |

For example, I want to be able to prove that u1 behaviour is different in forum 1 than in other forums and the particular thing about Forum 1 is that users in Forum 1 tend to have a higher value of the participation.

Does that make sense? What are the statistical techniques that I can use for verifying my hypothesis?
