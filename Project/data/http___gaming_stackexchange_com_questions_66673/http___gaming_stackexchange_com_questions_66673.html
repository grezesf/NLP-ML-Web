What does this blue asterisk/star indicate on top of an NPC?

up vote 5 down vote favorite

I've just informed the blacksmith that his apprentice is dead, and now he's got an odd icon over his head:

It looks like a blue asterisk or some kind of star. What does this symbol indicate?

It means they have information for you. If you click on them and select talk, it should provide you new options you haven't seen before (not grayed out).
