Can you put a matte polyurethane over a semi-gloss polyurethane to make it a matte finish?

up vote 3 down vote favorite

I have a piece of furniture that my mom has painted and she put on a semi-gloss polyurethane finish. It is way too shiny for the type of painting.

Can we put a matte polyurethane finish over the semi-gloss one to make it more of a matte or satin finish?

Yes (with some prep), however, you can try lightly sanding with 600 grit wet/dry sandpaper. Use it lightly wetted. Buff dry with a soft cloth. You may find this is matte enough.. DONE!

If its too matte (flat) you are now prepped and ready to recoat with satin or matte.
