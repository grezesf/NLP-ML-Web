Like Perry before me - I beleive that a good management can work (almost) any issue that comes up in a team. and yet - I beleive in human relations and intuition. terms that are often under-estimated in the business environment. I choose my people myself, interviewing them from the first stage, no "formal" techniques. In these interviews, I try to predict the following parameters:

  • How well does the person suit for my open position
  • How communicative she is, or more precisely, what kind of communication does she use? is this communication method(s) suitable for me and for the rest of the team?
  • How close she is to the other team members in age, social level, lifestyle, hobbies etc'.

I do not reject anyone according to this complliance, but i do think that there is a greater potential for team work if people can first be friends and only then co-workers.

In addition - I usually ask someone form the team to join the interview in order to have an additional opinion.
