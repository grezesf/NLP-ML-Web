How does skill damage scale in Cube World?

up vote 3 down vote favorite

The skill descriptions in Cube World outline effects and cooldowns, but they don't seem to outline damage.

How does skill damage scale in Cube World, and where can I see this scaling?

Your skills have a locked damage scaling. The damage you deal is tied to your equipment only. The only thing you get out of ranking most class skills is lower cooldown.

The exact damage scaling per skill is still unknown at this point.
