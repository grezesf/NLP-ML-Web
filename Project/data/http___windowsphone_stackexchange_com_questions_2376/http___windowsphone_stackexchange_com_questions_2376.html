I can't open new IE tabs on my WP7.5

up vote 2 down vote favorite

Bounty number TWO!

Because no one answered the first bounty, I am just going to blow my reputation points on second one. I am certain that one of you WP gurus has what it takes to help me resolve this issue.

Please post an answer only if you know the solution. Don't post "try this" types of answers. Those belong as comments. I don't want a non-answer to be selected by SE's auto-award system if it's not truly a helpful answer.

My question

Recently, IE on my WP7.5 has stopped opening new tabs. When I tap and hold a link, the context menu opens as normal, but tapping Open in new tab just causes the menu to close. Also, while in IE, I tap the ... to bring up the context menu, tap tabs, tap the (+) to create a new tab, a new tab icon appears as expected, but nothing happens. Neither tapping the tab nor the (x) to close it does anything. Something is definitely broken with my phone.

What can I do to fix this? I tried seeing if I could uninstall IE, but the option is not there, so so much for reinstalling it.

After further research, it appears that many other people are experiencing the same problem. As usual, it's just a bunch of people joining a multi-page discussion thread to say, "I am having the same issue. Has anyone found a fix?" Of course, no one has a solution other than the ones you don't want to try (eg, hard reset your phone which is absolutely ridiculous). One poster from the second link says they fixed it by editing the registry, but I cannot seem to find a registry editor that looks trustworthy. If you know of a good registry editor and this ultimately aids in the resolution of my issue, I will aware you the bounty.
