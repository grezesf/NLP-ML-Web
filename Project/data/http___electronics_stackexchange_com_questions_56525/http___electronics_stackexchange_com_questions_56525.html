In-line Sound Level Meter Circuit (with LED bar).

up vote -1 down vote favorite

I am looking to create an in-line sound level meter (indicator). This would connect between the headphone audio out (from a computer) and a pair of headphones. I do not need to get a precise measurement but want the sound level to be indicated on an LED bar. Ideally, I would like the top and bottom levels (the sound level that triggers the brightest green LED) to be configurable (possibly via a variable resistor).

I do have a component in mind for the 10 segment LED display: http://www.maplin.co.uk/10-segment-display-2128

I have already consulted Google - but I was looking for some practical advice.
