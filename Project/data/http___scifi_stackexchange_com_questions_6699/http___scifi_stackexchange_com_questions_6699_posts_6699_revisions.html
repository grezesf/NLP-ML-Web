What is the actual age of the following ponies:

  • Twilight Sparkle
  • Pinkie Pie
  • Rarity
  • Apple Jack
  • Rainbow Dash
  • Fluttershy
  • Princess Luna
  • Princess Celestia
  • Granny Smith

So let's add some constraints:

  • They live on their own.
  • Fluttershy is one year older than Pinkie Pie.
  • Princess Luna were banished to the moon for 1000 years.
  • Princess Celestia is older than Princess Luna.
  • Despite the fact that Granny Smith looks pretty old, Princess Luna and Celestia don't.
  • Spike is younger than Twilight Sparkle, I know that that Spike is not a pony.
  • We know that Twilight Sparkle has coursed magic kindergarten, so we can assume that she is at least 6 years old.
  • Since no pony is studying in a college we can assume that they're 17 or older.
  • After reviewing some chapters I found out that Rainbow Dash is at least 20% cooler that any other pony.

EDIT: In one episode 2 years ago, Rarity said something about Spike being 10. He must be 12 now then. -Errbay
