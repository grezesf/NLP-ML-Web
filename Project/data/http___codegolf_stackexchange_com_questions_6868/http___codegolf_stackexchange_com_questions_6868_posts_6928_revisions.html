The digits of the binary representation of Pi seem to behave as if they are chaotically produced [Bailey, D.H., and R.E. Crandall. "On the Random Character of Fundamental Constant Expansions." Experimental Mathematics 10, no. 2 (2001): 175-90.], although this is unproven.

I devised the The following simple routine to deterministically produce, returns as a string, the binary digits of pi, corresponding to d decimal digits:

f[d_]:=ToString@FromDigits@RealDigits[N[Pi,d],2][[1]]
